Profit Paths through Data:
Uncovering What Sells and What Scales

This Tableau dashboard offers a concise overview of product performance across 44 FMCG items in the Food & Beverages, Personal Care, and Pet Food categories. It tracks key metrics including gross revenue, profit, order quantity, profit margin, and discounts given. The dashboard enables quick, data-driven insights for optimizing product mix, pricing strategies, and overall category performance.

Research Analysis
using Power BI Artificial Intelligence visuals

A unique analytics project using Power BI’s Artificial Intelligence visual tools—Key Influencer Chart, Decomposition Tree, and Smart Narrative— to analyze 40+ years of global suicide data across 100+ countries by gender and generation class. Key findings: The Baby Boomers generation had the highest suicide count (2,283,228), over 14,000% more than Generation Z, and it accounted for 33.89% of total cases. Gender (male) and Generation (Boomers) were the top contributing factors to increased suicide numbers, highlighting critical demographic trends for mental health focus.

From Prescription to Performance:
A Deep Dive into Customer Metrics

This Power BI dashboard delivers a powerful, at-a-glance view of customer behavior and sales performance for a pharmaceutical company operating across 10 U.S. states. Designed for strategic decision-making, it tracks yearly totals, prior month figures, and month-over-month (MoM) % changes across four key metrics: revenue, quantity sold, number of customer orders, and active customer count. Interactive visuals bring insights to life—including a map of revenue by state, a Top 10 Customers leaderboard, and a revenue breakdown by customer type (e.g., hospitals, pharmacies, clinics). This dashboard equips commercial and sales teams with the intelligence to spot growth opportunities, understand regional performance, and tailor customer engagement strategies for maximum impact.
