Research Analysis
using Power BI Artificial Intelligence visuals

A unique analytics project using Power BI’s Artificial Intelligence visual tools—Key Influencer Chart, Decomposition Tree, and Smart Narrative— to analyze 40+ years of global suicide data across 100+ countries by gender and generation class. Key findings: The Baby Boomers generation had the highest suicide count (2,283,228), over 14,000% more than Generation Z, and it accounted for 33.89% of total cases. Gender (male) and Generation (Boomers) were the top contributing factors to increased suicide numbers, highlighting critical demographic trends for mental health focus.
